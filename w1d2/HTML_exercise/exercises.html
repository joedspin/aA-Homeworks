Apple Pie

  • Description

Desription

The greatest apple pie ever!


Wiki Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pastry Crust
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • cinammon

Directions

  1. Cut the apples.
  2. Make the crust
  3. Drizzle with olive oil
  4. Sprinkle the sugar
  5. Dash the cinammon
  6. Bake
